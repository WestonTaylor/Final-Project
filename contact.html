Main Adoption Event Location

Tractor supply in Tulare is the usual host of CSA's adoption events. These events typically take place on the first Saturday of each month, and run from 8am to 5pm.

Their address is: 1949 E Prosperity Ave, Tulare, CA 93274. Directions to these events are provided on the right side of the screen

Local Shelter Partner

CSA has been working with Porterville Animal Shelter (PAS) since the start. CSA chose to work with PAS because they only accept work through volunteers, and this ensures that those working there have a true passion for animals.

If you would also like to help out at PAS, their business hours are Monday to Friday 12-5pm, and Saturday 9am-5pm. You can volunteer to work at other times than this, but this is the best time to get ahold of someone at the shelter. Their address/location is provided on the right side of the screen.
