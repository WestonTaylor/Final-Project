Main Adoption Event Location

Tractor supply in Tulare is the usual host of CSA's adoption events. These events usually take place on the first Saturday of each month, and run from 8am to 5pm.

Directions to these events are provided on the right side of the screen

Partnered Local Shelter

CSA has been working with Porterville Animal Shelter (PAS) since the start. CSA chose to work with PAS because of they only accept work through volunteers.

If you would also like to help out at PAS, their hours of operation are Monday to Friday 12-5pm, and Saturday 9am-5pm. These are the hours which they let anyone come to the premises, but they will allow volunteers to come work hours with much greater flexibility. Directions to PAS are provided on the right side of the screen.
